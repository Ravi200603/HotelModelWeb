Kamloops Inn





"Established in 1901 by Mr. D. H. Campbell, the third mayor of Kamloops, our hotel proudly stands as the city's first-ever three-star establishment. A testament to our rich history and commitment to excellence, we have continuously evolved to set the standard for hospitality in Kamloops.

Our ethos extends beyond providing exceptional guest experiences. We are deeply rooted in the community, with a strong dedication to social responsibility. Each year, we allocate approximately 2% of our profits to support local food banks across British Columbia, including the Kamloops Food Bank. This initiative helps us provide essential food and clothing to those in need, reinforcing our commitment to making a positive impact in our community.

As we celebrate our heritage, we remain focused on delivering unparalleled service and contributing meaningfully to the wellbeing of our community."

- D.H. Campbell
